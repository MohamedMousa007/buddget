How it behaves
Tapping any numeric field opens the pad as a bottom sheet; the field stays visible above with a blinking caret and red focus ring. Keys type straight into the field — the OS keyboard never appears. Decimal is capped to 2 places, leading zeros are handled, backspace deletes one character, Done dismisses.
One component, every field
Reuse for expense & income amounts, debt / loan amounts, savings targets and budget limits (mode="decimal"), and for card last-4 / PIN entry (mode="pin", capped at 4, dot key hidden, dots readout). Pass variant to switch A/B and currency for the inline symbol.
